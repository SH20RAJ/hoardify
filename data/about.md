# About Hoardify

## 🌍 Who We Are

Hoardify is a technology-driven platform built to **transform outdoor advertising (OOH)**.  
We bring hoardings (billboards) online—making them **searchable, transparent, and measurable**.

In a world where digital ads are just a click away, outdoor advertising still remains **fragmented, manual, and opaque**. Hoardify exists to change that.

---

## 🚧 The Problem

Outdoor advertising today faces major challenges:

- No centralized platform to discover hoardings  
- Pricing is inconsistent and non-transparent  
- Booking involves multiple intermediaries  
- Campaign performance is difficult to measure  

This leads to **inefficiency, higher costs, and poor decision-making** for businesses.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Hoardify simplifies the entire process:

- 📍 **Discover hoardings on an interactive map**  
- 💰 **View transparent pricing and details**  
- ⚡ **Book or enquire instantly**  
- 📊 **Track campaign performance with analytics**  

We provide an **end-to-end platform** that connects advertisers, hoarding owners, and execution services.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

> To make outdoor advertising as simple, accessible, and data-driven as digital advertising.

---

## 🚀 What Makes Us Different

- **Map-first experience** for intuitive discovery
